Overview

Our team worked with AgBiz-Logic, a company that focuses on decision-making tools for agricultural producers, to build a functionality that calculates and records the Environmental Impact Quotient (EIQ) value for pesticides and feritlizers.

Technologies Used

  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • AngularJS
  • Python
  • Django
  • Selenium
